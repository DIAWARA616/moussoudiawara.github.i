Who I am

Environmental Engineer
based in Guinea

I am an Environmental Engineer with hands-on experience in the mining sector, water quality laboratory analysis, and academic environmental research. My work spans waste monitoring, site rehabilitation, environmental impact assessment (ESIA), and HSE compliance.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Environmental Sciences from the Université de N'Zérékoré and am currently completing my Master's degree. My academic research focuses on leachate composition from illegal dump sites — a critical public health and environmental issue in urban Guinea.

I am passionate about building the next generation of environmental governance in West Africa, one field study, one audit, and one community at a time.

Research & Publications

Research Report · 2024
Étude de la composition des lixiviats issus de décharges sauvages de la ville de N'Zérékoré
Université de N'Zérékoré, Guinea  ·  Unpublished academic research report
Investigation of leachate composition from illegal dump sites in the city of N'Zérékoré, Guinea. The study characterizes samples through physico-chemical and biological analyses and examines key influencing factors including waste type, climatic conditions, hydrology, and site geology. Includes environmental risk assessment and policy recommendations for urban waste governance.
